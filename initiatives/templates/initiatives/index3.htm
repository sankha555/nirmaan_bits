ABOUT
US

Nirmaan Organization is a registered NGO incepted by students of BITS Pilani in 2005 and works in the areas of Education, Community development, Women Empowerment, Employment generation, and Social Leadership. Since then, the organization has grown to work in several cities nationwide but the Pilani chapter still works with the people of villages around Pilani for their upliftment and operates from within the college campus.



OUR
VISION

To achieve a knowledge driven and an economically empowered society.



OUR
MISSION

To promote grassroots social innovations, volunteering spirit, active citizenship and social leadership among the youth of the nation.

We have only one

Passion,

OUR
PROJECTS


Gyan Bodh, Utkarsh, Shiksha ki Ore, Pilani Ki Pathshala & School Adoption Programme
Education is often referred to as the noblest of all professions. With our various education schemes, we embark upon a journey to end illiteracy. Gyan Bodh and SKO are our prime evening tuition schemes. Along with them, we have gone a step further with school adoption, taking the government school in Khelda Ka Baas under our wing overseeing it's renovation and development. Along with this in partnership with Aditya Birla group, we provide internships to deserving students.

Community development
To uplift the community on a whole we follow the policy of 'interact and expand'. We first spread our message and information to a few individuals and then with their help reach the masses.

Unnati & Employment Generation
Unnati's are our prime women empowerment and employment generation Our prime motive is to make the women skilled enough for employment. To suffice that, we are currently helping them develop products of sufficient regular use. Following that, we help them find a market for the products they skilfully develop. We also educate them on the various employment options available.

PUBLIC
RELATIONS
DEPARTMENT


The Public Relations Department or PRD, is the face of Nirmaan Organisation, Pilani chapter. PRD is divided into subsections which are content writing, videography, graphic designing, app, and website development. We work closely with the volunteers and alumni associated with Nirmaan and help create awareness about their work both inside and outside of campus.




OUR
EVENTS


Joy Of Giving Week

Joy of giving week is our flagship annual event. All the beneficiaries gather in our college campus for various fun and educational activities.

SORAD

Save One Rupee A Day (SORAD) is our annual flagship event and one of the biggest fund collection drives on campus in which hundreds of students and faculties pledge to save atleast one rupee everyday for a noble cause.

Punya

Punya is our bi-annual cycle collection and redistribution drive, benefitting thousands of underpriviledged students and communities.
